Gabriel Kondo is a male photographer, who explores all sides of photography, based and borned in São Paulo, Brazil. His interest in photography was kindled in college, were he started working with video production.

He started with street photography portraying the city that he loves, discovering new places and portraying everything that cought his attention.

With time, the development and maturation of his view of the world, he started searching for photographs with a more meaningful message.

Without abandoning his old curiosity and incorporating his new ideologies, he started to search for new interactions with the world.
